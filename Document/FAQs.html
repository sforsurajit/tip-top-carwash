Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services does Tip-Top Car Wash provide?
We offer exterior washing, interior cleaning, foam wash, detailing services, and specialized care for premium and luxury vehicles. Services are available at the customer’s location.
Do you provide door-to-door car wash services?
Yes. Tip-Top Car Wash operates primarily on a door-to-door service model, bringing professional car cleaning directly to your home or workplace.
How can I book a service?
You can book a service via phone call, WhatsApp, or through our website. Our team will confirm the appointment and service details.
How long does a car wash take?
Service duration depends on the selected package and vehicle condition. A standard wash typically takes 30–45 minutes, while detailing may take longer.
Do I need to provide water or electricity?
In most cases, we are self-sufficient. However, for certain services, access to water or electricity may be requested in advance.
Are your cleaning products safe for my vehicle?
Yes. We use vehicle-safe and eco-conscious cleaning products that are suitable for all types of cars, including premium and luxury vehicles.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, UPI, and other digital payment methods as communicated at the time of booking. Payment is due after service completion.
What if I am not satisfied with the service?
Please inform our staff immediately after service completion. We will review the concern and take appropriate corrective action as per our policy.
Do you offer subscription or regular wash plans?
Yes, we offer periodic and subscription-based service plans. Please contact us for current packages and pricing.
Do you operate in my area?
We currently operate in and around Kokrajhar, Assam. Service availability in nearby areas may vary.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
Yes. Cancellations or rescheduling requests should be made in advance. Late cancellations may attract charges as per our Terms & Conditions.
