About Tip-Top Car Wash

Tip-Top Car Wash is a modern, customer-focused car care service committed to delivering premium-quality vehicle cleaning with maximum convenience. Built around the concept of door-to-door service, we bring professional car wash and detailing solutions directly to our customers’ location.

Based in Kokrajhar, Assam, we are designed to serve individuals, families, and businesses who value time, quality, and consistent results. Our approach combines trained manpower, standardized processes, and safe cleaning materials to ensure every vehicle receives the care it deserves.

Our Mission
To redefine car care by delivering reliable, hygienic, and high-standard vehicle cleaning services at the customer’s doorstep.

What We Do

  • Exterior car washing
  • Interior cleaning and vacuuming
  • Foam wash and detailing
  • Premium and luxury vehicle care
  • Scheduled and on-demand services

Why Choose Tip-Top Car Wash

  • Trained and professional service staff
  • Convenient door-to-door service model
  • Safe, eco-conscious cleaning products
  • Transparent pricing and service packages
  • Quality checks and customer satisfaction focus

Our Values

  • Quality: Consistent, high-standard service delivery
  • Integrity: Honest pricing and transparent communication
  • Hygiene: Clean processes and safe handling
  • Customer First: Convenience and satisfaction at every step

Our Vision

To become a trusted and recognizable car care brand in the region by setting benchmarks in service quality, reliability, and customer experience.
